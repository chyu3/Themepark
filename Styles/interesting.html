Map of the Park!

Start off your journey with the Crossroad of Destiny into the Enchanted Jungle, where many dreams are said to have vanished, take part in the River of Adventure, and brave the rapids as you make your way down to the end of this cursed river. Next, go towards the Ancient Aztec and enter the world famous Pyramid of the Ancients, where it is said that many lost souls wander, a famous Desert themed horror maze, and then into the Joyful Circus, where everyone is always laughing, no matter what. Then, after cooling off with a shower during the middle of the day, head into the Sci-Fi land, to ride the 1000ft rollercoaster, and into the "zero-gravity" loop, where physics are said to be defied. Catch a photo of the satellite mid-air to stand a chance to win exciting prizes! Finally, to end off your day, go towards the Refurbishments of the Wanderers to satiate your famished appetite!
